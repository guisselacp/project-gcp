Concepts

What is diversity in the workplace?

Diversity in the workplace means that a company hires a wide range of diverse individuals. Diversity is often misconceived as solely multicultural matters, however, it also applies to a diversity of gender, race, ethnicity, age, sexuality, language, education, background, and so on.

But now, workplace diversity doesn't just extend to hiring diverse individuals, but also making sure that the participation of these employees is equal. More importantly, companies have recognised the huge value that hiring a diverse range of individuals and personalities can add for their business.

What is the importance of diversity in the workplace?

Diversity and inclusion are a vital part of workplace culture; not only are they important for the well-being of a company’s employees, but will also benefit the business itself.

Understanding that globalization has caused the positive cultural chasms that have created culturally diverse teams is part of the equation that organizations need to face.
